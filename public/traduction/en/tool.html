The 3d imprimante

Where did this come from?

in July 1984 the first patent for 3d printing is filed, then in August a patent is filed by Americans. In 1988 the first 3d printer is launched in 2006 open source 3d printer project for future home 3d printers

What's that for?

The 3d printer is used to create a multitude of objects with a great variety of material choices according to the desired result, it allows to create from the simple vase to the most complex mechanisms, nowadays they even print houses!3d printing has enabled associations such as E-nable, whose aim is to make prostheses more accessible, to distribute hundreds of prostheses.

How much does it cost?

3d prints can cost various prices from 150€ to 1000€ and then you have to count 250€ to make your personalized prosthesis, but it's cheaper to do it by printer than by other means.

Materials

For 3d printers you can use plastic, aluminium, polyamide powder (from the reaction of a polyacid on a polyamine), aluminium powder (polyamide and aluminium).

The factory

The only other known way is the manufacture, and yes the human hand remains a solid tool! However it takes time (it takes 2 weeks to a month for a dental prosthesis) and it also costs money it can go up to $40,000 in America.
